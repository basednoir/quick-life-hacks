Home Hacks

Bread to Pick Up Glass:

Press a slice of soft bread over shattered glass shards to collect the tiny, invisible ones your broom missed.

Towel in the Freezer for Summer Nights:

Wet a towel, wring it out, toss it in the freezer, and use it as a chill pillow topper for hot evenings.

Rubber Band Paint Scraper:

Stretch a rubber band across an open paint can to wipe excess off your brush—keeps the rim clean and paint usable.

Socks on a Broom = Floor Duster:

Slip an old sock over a broom head to easily dust under furniture.

Use Chalk to Prevent Mold in Closets:

Toss a few sticks of chalk in a mesh bag—they absorb excess moisture and reduce mildew.

Alka-Seltzer to Clean the Toilet Bowl:

Drop two in, wait 15 minutes, brush—it’s fizz-powered scrubbing without effort.

WD-40 to Remove Crayon from Walls:

Spray a little on crayon marks, wipe with a cloth—wall art gone.

Ice Cubes to Lift Carpet Dents:

Place ice cubes where furniture dented the carpet, let them melt, then fluff with a fork.
