Clothes Hacks

Ice Cubes in Dryer to De-Wrinkle:

Toss 1–2 in with wrinkled clothes and run on high for 10 mins—wrinkles steam right out.

Shaving Cream for Makeup Stains:

Dab it on lipstick/foundation stains and blot—great emergency fix.

Rubber Glove for Pet Hair:

Put one on and swipe over clothes or furniture—the static grabs all the hair.

Hair Straightener as Collar Iron:

Perfect for touching up shirt collars or hems in a pinch.

Freeze Jeans to De-Stink:

Pop your jeans in a freezer bag overnight to kill odor-causing bacteria without washing.
