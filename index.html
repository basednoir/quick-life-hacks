Unexpected tricks you’ll wish you knew sooner!

Featured Hacks

  • Banana Peel for Splinters: Tape the inside of a banana peel over a splinter overnight—the enzymes help draw it out like magic.
  • Aluminum Foil to Sharpen Scissors: Snip through folded aluminum foil 8–10 times to bring dull scissors back to life.
  • Tennis Ball for Jar Opening: Cut a tennis ball in half and use the inside as a grippy, palm-friendly jar opener.
  • Toothpaste on Headlights: Rub non-gel toothpaste on foggy headlights, scrub with a sponge, rinse—and boom, instant clarity.
  • Dryer Sheet in Stinky Shoes: Pop a dryer sheet in each shoe overnight to banish odor (bonus: smells like fresh laundry feet).
