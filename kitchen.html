Kitchen Hacks

Lettuce for Fresh Brown Sugar:

Toss a clean lettuce leaf into a container of rock-hard brown sugar—softens overnight without added moisture.

Microwave Citrus for More Juice:

Nuke a lemon for 10 seconds before squeezing to get way more juice with less effort.

Plastic Bottle Egg Separator:

Crack an egg into a bowl, then suck up the yolk using an empty plastic bottle—cheap, weird, wildly effective.

Use Frozen Grapes as Wine Chillers:

Keep wine cold without watering it down—just drop in frozen grapes.

Flip a Muffin Tin for Mini Taco Bowls:

Turn your muffin tin upside down, drape tortillas over the bottoms, and bake for DIY taco shells.

Boil Water Before Freezing for Clear Ice Cubes:

Crystal-clear ice cubes = impressively bougie drinks.

Use a Paper Towel to Keep Greens Crisp:

Stick one in your salad container—it absorbs moisture and prevents sogginess.

Mason Jar + Blender Hack:

Screw a regular mason jar onto most blender bases to blend single servings (great for smoothies or dressings).
