Car Hacks

Shoe Organizer on the Back of the Seat:

Great for storing snacks, chargers, tissues—mom car magic.

Cereal Container as Trash Can:

Line a plastic cereal container with a bag and keep it in your car for tidy toss-outs.

Toothpaste to Polish Headlights:

It’s worth repeating—cheap, weird, and works.

Keep Dryer Sheets Under Seats:

Car smells fresh without that artificial pine tree smell.

Socks Over Wipers to Prevent Icy Windshields:

If snow is coming, slip socks over your wipers at night—they won’t freeze to the glass.
