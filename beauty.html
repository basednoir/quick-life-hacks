Beauty Hacks

Deodorant to Prevent Blisters:

Rub it on heels or sides of your feet where shoes rub—prevents friction.

Green Tea Bags for Puffy Eyes:

Steep, chill in the fridge, then place over your eyes—bye, bags.

Toothbrush for Flyaway Fix:

Spray a clean toothbrush with hairspray and gently smooth baby hairs.

Dry Shampoo Overnight Trick:

Apply before bed, not in the morning—it works better as it absorbs oil while you sleep.
